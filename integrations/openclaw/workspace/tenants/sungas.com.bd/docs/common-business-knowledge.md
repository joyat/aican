## 1. Company Snapshot

Sun Gas Limited is a Bangladesh LPG company focused on importing, bottling, and distributing LPG across domestic, commercial, industrial, and autogas segments. Public materials describe the company as one of the larger LPG sales and distribution operators in Bangladesh and note that it operates under the domain `sungas.com.bd`.

Public company profiles and job postings indicate:

- headquarters presence in **Gulshan, Dhaka**
- operating activity tied to **Mongla Port / Mongla industrial area, Bagerhat**
- nationwide distribution reach across Bangladesh
- active hiring across finance, operations, sales, audit, and project roles

## 2. Public Background

Public profiles describe Sun Gas Limited as:

- a **multinational LPG importing and bottling company**
- operating in Bangladesh since **2001**
- previously associated with **LAUGFS Gas BD / LAUGFS Gas** branding before rebranding to **Sun Gas**

Public LinkedIn content states that Sun Gas Limited is under the ownership of **Kai Heng Long Global Energy, Hong Kong** and that a rebranding from LAUGFS Gas BD was underway.

Because these facts come from public profile and hiring materials rather than an audited annual report in this repository, they should be treated as **public background context**, not as internally approved legal wording.

## 3. Operations Footprint

Public Bangladesh job postings and public registry pages indicate two main visible operating footprints:

### Dhaka office

Public job posts list the office at:

**South Avenue Tower (3rd/4th Floor), House #50, Road #3, 7 Gulshan Avenue, Gulshan, Dhaka-1212, Bangladesh**

This appears to function as the corporate / commercial office used for recruiting and management roles.

### Mongla operations

Public materials describe a **world-class LPG import, bottling, and distribution facility** at or near **Mongla Port / Mongla industrial area, Bagerhat**.

Public descriptions commonly mention:

- LPG import and bottling operations
- storage capacity of around **5,000 MT**
- nationwide distribution support

The Bangladesh government labour inspection registry also shows registered Sun Gas sites in both **Mongla, Bagerhat** and **Gulshan, Dhaka**.
