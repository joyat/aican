## About Sungas Technologies

Sungas Technologies Ltd. was founded in 2011 by CEO **Arjun Mehta** and CTO **Li Wei Chen** — two former enterprise software engineers who met at Salesforce's Singapore office and identified an unmet need for intelligent, compliance-aware SaaS platforms tailored to Asia-Pacific mid-market enterprises.

The company launched its flagship **SunCloud Platform** in 2013, targeting regional banks and insurers struggling to migrate legacy data workflows to the cloud. By 2016, Sungas had expanded to India (Bangalore R&D hub) and Australia (Sydney commercial office), signing its first Fortune 500 contract with ANZ Banking Group.

A USD 18M Series B in 2018 funded the London office and accelerated the push into EMEA. In 2020, Sungas launched **SunAI Analytics** — a decision-intelligence layer built on proprietary ML pipelines — which now accounts for 37% of ARR.

In 2022, the Dubai office opened to serve GCC financial services clients. That same year Sungas acquired UK-based **CipherNest Ltd.** for GBP 8.2M, folding its data-loss prevention technology into the new **SunShield Security** product line.

As of 2025, Sungas operates across **5 countries** with approximately **650 employees** and **SGD 94M annual recurring revenue**.

## Products

### SunCloud Platform
Enterprise cloud data management platform. Core modules: DataVault (secure multi-cloud storage), WorkflowEngine (low-code process automation), IntegrationHub (500+ pre-built connectors). Serves ~180 enterprise clients across BFSI, healthcare, and government sectors.

### SunAI Analytics
Predictive analytics and decision-intelligence suite. Modules: InsightDash (executive dashboards), ForecastAI (ML-based forecasting), RiskRadar (real-time anomaly detection). Contributes 37% of ARR as of FY2025.

### SunShield Security
Enterprise data security and compliance platform, incorporating CipherNest DLP technology. Key capabilities: data classification, regulatory mapping (MAS TRM, GDPR, ISO 27001), zero-trust policy enforcement. Fastest-growing product line at +68% YoY in FY2024.
