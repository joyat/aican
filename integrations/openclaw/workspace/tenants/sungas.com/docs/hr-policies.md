## 1. Equal Opportunity and Inclusion

Sungas is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, religion, national origin, gender, gender identity, age, disability, marital status, family responsibilities, or any other protected characteristic.

We are committed to building a culturally diverse team that reflects our global client base. Our workforce spans 22 nationalities. We have an active Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI) working group that reports to the CPO on a quarterly basis.

**Grievances related to discrimination or harassment** should be reported to your HR Business Partner or to the anonymous Ethics Hotline at ethicshotline.sungas.com. All reports are investigated promptly and confidentially.

---

## 2. Anti-Harassment Policy

Sungas has zero tolerance for harassment, whether sexual, racial, religious, or based on any other protected characteristic. Harassment includes:
- Unwanted physical contact
- Verbal abuse, insults, or degrading comments
- Inappropriate messages, images, or memes in work channels
- Creating a hostile environment for a colleague

Any employee found to have harassed a colleague will face disciplinary action up to and including immediate termination.

## 3. Recruitment and Onboarding

### Job Postings
All open roles are posted on the Sungas careers page (careers.sungas.com) and on LinkedIn, JobStreet, and Glassdoor. Employee referrals are encouraged — the referral bonus is SGD 2,000 (Singapore and Bangalore) or local equivalent, paid 3 months after the referred employee's start date.

### Onboarding
New employees complete a structured 30-day onboarding programme:
- Day 1: IT setup, device collection, access credentials
- Days 1–5: Role-specific orientation sessions with team
- Days 5–15: Product overview sessions with all three product teams
- Day 30: 30-day check-in with manager and HR Business Partner

Each new employee is assigned an onboarding buddy — a peer from the same team who has been with Sungas for at least 12 months.

### Probation Period
All new employees serve a 3-month probation period (6 months in Singapore for management roles). Performance during probation is reviewed at 4 weeks and at completion. Employees who do not pass probation receive 2 weeks' notice.

## 4. Performance Management

### Quarterly Check-Ins
Every employee has a 30-minute quarterly check-in with their line manager. Format: (1) review of goals and priorities, (2) feedback exchange, (3) development discussion. Notes recorded in Workday.

### Annual Performance Review
Conducted in October–November. Rating scale:
- 5 — Exceptional: Significantly exceeded all expectations
- 4 — Strong: Exceeded most expectations; a top performer
- 3 — Meets expectations: Solid performance, all goals delivered
- 2 — Partially meets expectations: Some goals not met; improvement plan required
- 1 — Does not meet expectations: Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) initiated

**Performance Improvement Plans (PIPs):** Employees rated 2 or below receive a 60-day PIP with specific, measurable targets. Continued underperformance after a PIP may result in termination.

## 5. Career Development

### Learning & Development Budget
Every employee receives SGD 1,500/year for learning and development. Eligible spend includes:
- Professional certifications (AWS, GCP, CFA, ACCA, PMI, etc.)
- Online courses (Coursera, Udemy, Pluralsight, LinkedIn Learning)
- Conference attendance (including travel costs with manager approval)
- Technical books and resources

Requests above SGD 300 require manager pre-approval via Workday. Budget resets on 1 January each year; unused balances do not carry forward.

### Internal Mentorship
The Sungas Mentorship Programme matches employees with a senior mentor from a different function. Applications are open in Q1 each year; programme runs for 6 months with structured monthly check-ins. Apply via people@sungas.com.

### Cross-Functional Rotations
Employees who have been with Sungas for at least 18 months may apply for 3–6 month cross-functional rotations. Past rotations have included Bangalore engineers spending time with the Singapore Customer Success team, and Sydney commercial staff spending time with the Bangalore product team. Apply via your HR Business Partner.

## 6. Compensation Philosophy (HR-Restricted)

*This section is for HR and manager reference only and should not be shared with employees outside the HR function.*

Sungas positions total compensation (base + bonus + ESOP) at the **60th–75th percentile of market** for roles in each location, benchmarked annually against Radford/Willis Towers Watson data for the enterprise SaaS sector.

**Salary bands by level (Singapore, FY2025 reference):**

| Grade | Level | Base range (SGD/year) |
|-------|-------|----------------------|
| G1 | Associate | 48,000–65,000 |
| G2 | Senior Associate | 65,000–88,000 |
| G3 | Senior / Specialist | 88,000–120,000 |
| G4 | Manager / Lead | 120,000–165,000 |
| G5 | Director | 165,000–220,000 |
| G6 | VP | 220,000–320,000 |
| G7 | C-Suite | 320,000+ |

Engineering levels (Bangalore equivalents are adjusted 55–60% of Singapore bands to reflect local market rates).

**Annual Bonus:** Target bonus as % of base salary by grade: G1–G2: 5%, G3: 8%, G4: 12%, G5: 15%, G6: 20%, G7: 25–40%. Paid in February for the preceding year's performance.

## 7. Termination Policy

### Voluntary Resignation
Employees must provide notice as per their employment contract:
- G1–G3: 1 month
- G4–G5: 2 months
- G6–G7: 3 months

Garden leave may be applied at the company's discretion.

### Involuntary Termination
Sungas follows a progressive discipline process except in cases of gross misconduct. Gross misconduct includes: theft, fraud, data breach, harassment, and serious safety violations. In gross misconduct cases, employment may be terminated immediately following a fair investigation.

### Exit Process
Departing employees must return all company assets (laptop, access cards) on or before their last day. IT will disable all system access on the last day. Finance will process final salary and expense reimbursement within 5 business days of exit.
