## 1. Financial Reporting Structure

Sungas Technologies Ltd. is the Singapore-incorporated holding entity. Subsidiary entities:
- Sungas Technologies India Pvt. Ltd. (Bangalore — Indian entity, IGST-registered)
- Sungas Technologies Ltd. (UK Branch — VAT-registered, HMRC-reporting)
- Sungas Technologies Australia Pty Ltd. (GST-registered, ASIC-registered)
- Sungas Technologies Ltd. (DIFC Branch — UAE, DIFC-regulated)

**Group consolidation** is prepared monthly in NetSuite by the Finance Controller (Benjamin Lim), reviewed by CFO (Priya Nair), and presented to the CEO and Board by the 10th business day of the following month.

**Functional currency:** SGD for the Singapore entity. Each subsidiary reports in local currency and is translated at the month-end closing rate for consolidation.

## 3. Accounts Payable (AP)

### Vendor Invoices
All vendor invoices must be sent to ap@sungas.com. Do not forward to personal inboxes.

**Invoice requirements:**
- Vendor legal name and address
- GST/VAT registration number (where applicable)
- Invoice number and date
- Sungas entity name (confirm which entity the PO was raised against)
- Line item description, quantity, unit price, and total
- Bank details (must match pre-approved vendor master)

**Approval thresholds:**

| Invoice Value (SGD equivalent) | Approval Required |
|-------------------------------|-------------------|
| Up to SGD 5,000 | Department manager |
| SGD 5,001 – SGD 25,000 | Department VP |
| SGD 25,001 – SGD 100,000 | CFO |
| Above SGD 100,000 | CFO + CEO |

**Payment terms:** Standard net-30 from invoice receipt date. Vendors requesting early payment discounts must submit to ap@sungas.com with subject line "Early Payment Request".

**Payment runs:** Twice weekly — Tuesday and Thursday. Payments processed in local currency via the entity's operating bank account. FX transactions above SGD 50,000 require CFO authorisation.

## 4. Accounts Receivable (AR)

**Invoicing:** Customer invoices are raised by the Finance team in NetSuite upon confirmation of contract milestones (for project-based contracts) or subscription renewal dates (for SaaS ARR contracts). Sales Operations is responsible for ensuring Salesforce opportunity stages are updated to trigger invoicing workflows.

**Credit terms:** Standard net-30 for enterprise clients. Exceptions (net-45, net-60) require VP Sales and CFO approval.

**Collections:** Finance Controller manages collections. Accounts at 45+ days overdue are flagged to the relevant Customer Success Manager for joint outreach. Accounts at 90+ days overdue are escalated to the CFO and referred to Legal if required.

**Bad debt provisioning:** Reviewed quarterly. Provision raised at 25% for balances 90–180 days overdue; 100% for 180+ days overdue.

## 5. Expense Reimbursement Policy

### Submission
All expense claims must be submitted via Workday within **30 days** of the expense being incurred. Claims submitted after 90 days require CFO approval and are not guaranteed to be reimbursed.

### Eligible Expenses
- Business travel (flights, accommodation, ground transport — see limits below)
- Client entertainment (meals, events — see limits below)
- Home office setup (one-time SGD 400 for new hires on hybrid/remote arrangements, pre-approved)
- Professional subscriptions directly relevant to work (pre-approved by manager)

### Travel Limits

| Category | Economy Policy | Exceptions |
|----------|---------------|------------|
| Flights < 5 hours | Economy | None |
| Flights ≥ 5 hours | Economy | Director+ may claim Business Class with VP approval |
| C-Suite flights | Business Class | - |
| Hotels (Singapore) | Up to SGD 280/night | CFO approval required above |
| Hotels (London, Sydney) | Up to SGD 350/night | - |
| Hotels (other) | Up to SGD 220/night | - |
| Daily meals (no client) | Up to SGD 60/day | - |
| Client entertainment | Up to SGD 120/person per occasion | VP approval above |

### Non-Reimbursable Items
- Alcohol (unless part of an approved client entertainment claim with receipts)
- Personal entertainment, in-room movies, mini-bar
- Fines or penalties
- Personal travel extensions
- First-class flights under any circumstance

## 6. Purchase Orders (PO) and Procurement

All vendor engagements above SGD 5,000 require a Purchase Order raised in NetSuite before the engagement commences. Retroactive POs are not accepted without Finance Director sign-off.

**How to raise a PO:**
1. Submit a Procurement Request in Workday (Procurement > New Request)
2. Include: vendor name, scope of work, estimated cost, timeline, budget code
3. Manager approval is triggered automatically; Finance team issues PO once approved
4. PO number must be on every invoice from that vendor

**Vendor Onboarding:** New vendors must complete the Sungas Vendor Registration Form (available on Google Drive) and provide bank details, company registration, and Tax ID before any payment can be processed.

## 7. Budget Management

Annual budgets are set in November–December for the following fiscal year. The process:
1. Finance issues budget templates to all department heads (November)
2. Departments submit draft budgets by 1 December
3. CFO reviews, challenges, and aligns with CEO
4. Board approves final budget in December Board meeting
5. Approved budgets are loaded into NetSuite by 10 January

**Mid-year reforecasting:** Conducted after Q2 close (June). Material variances (>10% on any line item >SGD 50,000) require CFO notification.

**Budget codes:** All expenses must be coded to the correct cost centre and GL code. Cost centre structure: CC-SGP (Singapore HQ), CC-BLR (Bangalore), CC-LDN (London), CC-SYD (Sydney), CC-DXB (Dubai).

---

## 8. Audit and Compliance

**Internal Audit:** Finance conducts a quarterly internal review of high-risk control areas (AP duplicate payments, expense policy compliance, access controls to financial systems). Results reported to the Audit Committee.

**External Audit:** PwC Singapore serves as the group auditor. Annual audit commences in mid-January. All staff must cooperate fully with auditor requests and are reminded that providing misleading information to auditors is a serious disciplinary offence.

**ACRA Filing (Singapore):** Annual return and audited accounts filed by 30 April each year.

**GST (Singapore):** Quarterly GST returns filed with IRAS. Finance Controller is responsible for preparation; CFO signs off.
