## About Moongas Industries

Moongas Industries AG was founded in 1987 by **Klaus-Dieter Hoffmann** in Stuttgart, initially as a small-batch CNC machining workshop serving the Baden-Württemberg automotive supplier network. Through rigorous investment in tooling, metrology, and process certification, the company grew steadily through the 1990s into a preferred Tier 2 supplier to Daimler, Bosch, and ZF Friedrichshafen.

In 1998, Moongas received its first ISO 9001 certification and opened a second facility in Stuttgart-Feuerbach, tripling precision component capacity. The company entered industrial automation in 2004 with the launch of the **MoonAuto** assembly systems division, designing bespoke automated production lines for European manufacturers.

International expansion began in 2007 with a manufacturing joint venture in **Detroit, Michigan**, serving North American automotive OEMs. A **Tokyo** engineering and sales office followed in 2010, deepening the partnership with Japanese electronics and robotics manufacturers. In 2013, Moongas opened a **São Paulo** plant to serve Brazilian heavy industry and energy customers.

The acquisition of **PolyCast Sp. z o.o.** in Warsaw in 2017 (for EUR 34M) brought advanced aluminium and titanium casting capabilities into the group, and the **Warsaw** site now houses the company's specialty alloys division. A **Singapore** sales and application engineering office opened in 2020 to coordinate ASEAN industrial contracts.

In 2023, Moongas Industries AG completed a EUR 120M capital investment in its Stuttgart headquarters, installing a fully connected Industry 4.0 production floor — real-time OEE dashboards, predictive maintenance, and MES integration across all six sites.

As of 2025, Moongas operates across **6 countries** with approximately **1,200 employees** and **EUR 487M annual revenue**.

## Products and Divisions

### Precision CNC Components
High-tolerance machined parts for automotive, aerospace, medical, and energy sectors. Tolerances to ±2 microns. Materials: steel alloys, aluminium, titanium, Inconel. Key certifications: IATF 16949, AS9100D, ISO 13485.

### MoonAuto Assembly Systems
Custom industrial automation and assembly line design. Capabilities: robotic cell integration (Fanuc, KUKA, ABB), vision inspection systems, end-of-line testing rigs, MES connectivity. Delivered over 340 turnkey automation projects across 18 countries.

### Specialty Alloys (PolyCast Division)
Advanced aluminium and titanium castings for lightweight structural applications. Warsaw facility. Customers include aerospace primes and next-generation EV battery enclosure manufacturers.
