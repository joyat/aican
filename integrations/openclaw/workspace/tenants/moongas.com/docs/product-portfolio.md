## Overview

Moongas Industries operates three product divisions. Each serves distinct industry verticals but shares the company's core capabilities in precision manufacturing, materials expertise, and quality systems.

**FY2024 Revenue Contribution:**
- Precision CNC Components: 52% (EUR ~253M)
- MoonAuto Assembly Systems: 31% (EUR ~151M)
- Specialty Alloys (PolyCast): 17% (EUR ~83M)

## 1. Precision CNC Components

### What We Make
High-tolerance machined components for safety-critical applications in automotive, aerospace, medical devices, and energy. Parts are produced from a range of materials on multi-axis CNC machining centres, with full in-process and final inspection.

### Capabilities

**Materials Processed:**
- Steel alloys (including hardened tool steel, spring steel, stainless)
- Aluminium alloys (6000 and 7000 series)
- Titanium (Grade 2, Grade 5 / Ti-6Al-4V)
- Inconel and other nickel superalloys (aerospace and energy applications)
- Brass and copper alloys (precision electronics components)

**Dimensional Tolerances:**
- Standard: ±0.01 mm
- Precision: ±0.005 mm
- Ultra-precision (Stuttgart, dedicated CMM cells): ±0.002 mm (2 microns)

**Equipment:**
- 147 CNC machining centres (Mazak, DMG Mori, Okuma, Fanuc-controlled) across Stuttgart, Feuerbach, Detroit, and São Paulo
- 5-axis simultaneous machining: 38 machines (Stuttgart and Warsaw)
- 12 CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine) cells (Zeiss Contura, Hexagon)
- Automated production lines with robotic loading/unloading (MoonAuto-built in-house)

### Quality Certifications for This Division
IATF 16949, AS9100D, ISO 13485, ISO 9001

### Key Industry Verticals and Applications

**Automotive:**
Transmission housings, valve bodies, brake callipers, steering rack components, fuel system precision fittings, EV motor housings, battery management module housings. Customers include Bosch, ZF, Continental, Valeo, and several European EV manufacturers.

**Aerospace:**
Structural brackets, actuation system components, hydraulic manifolds, engine accessory housing components. Supplied to Airbus supply chain (via Warsaw) and European defense prime contractors.

**Medical Devices:**
Surgical instrument components, orthopaedic implant structures, diagnostic equipment housings. Stuttgart facility only (ISO 13485 certification). Strict traceability: every part has a unique serial number and full manufacturing trace record retained for 15 years.

**Energy:**
High-pressure valve components, pump housings, subsea connector bodies for oil & gas and renewable energy infrastructure. São Paulo plant specialises in energy sector components.

## 2. MoonAuto Assembly Systems

### What We Do
MoonAuto designs, builds, commissions, and supports complete turnkey industrial automation and assembly systems. We are a systems integrator, not a robot vendor — we take full project responsibility from concept to handover and ongoing support.

### Capabilities

**Robotic Cell Integration:**
- Partner integrators for Fanuc, KUKA, ABB, and Universal Robots platforms
- Collaborative robot (cobot) applications for mixed human-robot assembly
- Custom end-of-arm tooling (EOAT) designed in-house for part-specific handling

**Vision and Inspection Systems:**
- Cognex and Keyence vision system integration for dimensional inspection, label verification, and assembly correctness checking
- AI-assisted defect detection (MoonVision module, introduced 2023): ML-based visual inspection that adapts to new part variants without manual reprogramming

**Assembly Line Engineering:**
- Cycle-time analysis and process simulation before build (Siemens Plant Simulation)
- MES (Manufacturing Execution System) integration — all lines deliver real-time OEE data to the customer's factory management system
- Poka-yoke (mistake-proofing) engineering is mandatory on all MoonAuto lines

**Testing and End-of-Line (EOL) Systems:**
- Functional test rigs for hydraulic, pneumatic, electrical, and mechanical assemblies
- Leak testing (pressure decay, helium), torque verification, electrical circuit validation
- 100% traceability: each unit's test results linked to its serial/barcode record

**After-Sales Support:**
- 24/7 remote monitoring service (MoonConnect platform): real-time line health visibility for customers
- Preventive maintenance contracts (PMC): quarterly on-site visits + 24-hour response SLA
- Spare parts: 48-hour delivery for standard components from Stuttgart warehouse

### Project Scale
- Smallest projects: single robotic cell, EUR 120K–EUR 350K
- Mid-range: multi-station assembly line, EUR 500K–EUR 3M
- Large turnkey plants: EUR 5M–EUR 25M, 12–24 month delivery
- Largest delivered: EUR 18M automotive body assembly plant, Germany, 2022

**Delivered to date:** 342 projects across 18 countries (as of April 2025)

## 3. Specialty Alloys — PolyCast Division (Warsaw)

### What We Make
The Warsaw facility (operating as the PolyCast division since the 2017 acquisition) produces high-integrity aluminium and titanium castings for aerospace, defence, and emerging EV applications. PolyCast holds NADCAP certification for casting special processes — one of fewer than 40 casting facilities in Europe with this accreditation.

### Casting Processes

**Investment Casting (Lost-Wax):**
Complex near-net-shape components with excellent surface finish. Tolerances to ±0.1 mm on critical dimensions. Used for aerospace structural components, turbine airfoils (non-rotating), and complex hydraulic manifolds.

**High-Pressure Die Casting (HPDC):**
High-volume aluminium structural components. Primary process for EV battery enclosures. New 3,200-tonne die casting press installed 2024 (EUR 14M investment) to support Project Moonrise (EV battery enclosure supply agreement).

**Low-Pressure Casting:**
Medium-complexity structural and semi-structural aluminium parts. Used for automotive structural components and industrial pump housings.

**Titanium Casting:**
Specialist capability. Used for aerospace and medical implant applications. Vacuum furnace environment required. Low-volume, high-value programme (approximately 8% of Warsaw revenue).

### Materials
- Aluminium alloys: A356, 6061, AlSi10Mg (additive and cast variants)
- Titanium: Ti-6Al-4V (Grade 5), Ti-6Al-4V ELI (medical grade)

### Quality Certifications for This Division
NADCAP (Casting), AS9100D, ISO 9001, EN 9100, IATF 16949

### Key Programmes
- Airbus A320/A321 structural bracket supply (ongoing since 2019)
- Project Moonrise: EV battery enclosure castings for European EV manufacturer (EUR 180M, 5-year programme, commenced 2024)
- Multiple aerospace defence programmes (details under NDA)

## 5. Technology and Innovation

**Industry 4.0 (Stuttgart HQ — 2023 upgrade):**
Full MES deployment (SAP Digital Manufacturing), real-time OEE dashboards per machine and cell, predictive maintenance via vibration and temperature sensors on all spindles, digital twin programme for complex part families.

**MoonVision (AI Visual Inspection, 2023):**
ML-based inspection system developed by the MoonAuto team. Reduced false rejection rate on complex visual inspection tasks by 34% vs. rule-based vision systems. Currently deployed on 8 customer lines and in two Stuttgart production cells.

**Sustainability:**
Moongas has committed to a 40% reduction in Scope 1 and Scope 2 CO₂ emissions by 2030 (2019 baseline). Key initiatives: solar installation on Stuttgart Feuerbach roof (completed 2023, 1.2 MW), switch to green electricity tariffs for Stuttgart HQ (2024), coolant recycling programme (>90% recovery across Stuttgart sites).
