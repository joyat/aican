## 1. Welcome

Welcome to Moongas Industries. This handbook sets out the policies, standards, and expectations that apply to all Moongas employees, regardless of site or function. It is your primary reference for how we operate as a company.

Where local employment law or a site-specific Works Council agreement (Betriebsvereinbarung) differs from this handbook, local law and site agreements prevail. Employees in Germany are also covered by the Works Constitution Act (Betriebsverfassungsgesetz).

For questions not answered here, contact your local HR Business Partner or email hr@moongas.com.

---

## 2. Our Values

Moongas was built on engineering discipline and the belief that doing things correctly is always worth the time it takes.

1. **Precision in everything** — Tolerance for error is zero on the production floor. We bring the same discipline to our communications, decisions, and commitments.
2. **Engineered for the long term** — We optimise for decade-long durability in products, partnerships, and people.
3. **Trust through transparency** — Customers, suppliers, and colleagues deserve accurate information, communicated clearly and on time.
4. **Safety without compromise** — No delivery schedule, cost target, or management instruction justifies bypassing a safety or quality procedure.
5. **Continuous improvement (Kaizen mindset)** — Every process can be improved. Every employee is empowered to raise an improvement idea.

### Production Shifts (Stuttgart, Feuerbach, Detroit, São Paulo, Warsaw)
Production facilities operate up to three shifts. Shift patterns are posted in the facility notice board and in the site MES system. Shift changes require a minimum of 5 working days' notice unless agreed differently by the Works Council (German sites).

### Overtime
Overtime must be pre-approved by the line manager. German sites: overtime compensation follows the applicable collective agreement (Tarifvertrag Metall- und Elektroindustrie). Other sites: follow local employment law. Unauthorised overtime will not be compensated.

## 4. Leave and Absence

### Annual Leave

| Site | Annual leave entitlement |
|------|--------------------------|
| Germany (all sites) | 30 working days (per Bundesurlaubsgesetz minimum + company top-up) |
| USA (Detroit) | 15 days/year, increasing to 20 after 5 years |
| Brazil (São Paulo) | 30 days/year per CLT |
| Japan (Tokyo) | 20 days/year (10 statutory + 10 company top-up) |
| Poland (Warsaw) | 26 days/year (10+ years service) or 20 days |
| Singapore | 18 days/year |

Leave applications must be submitted in the Moongas HR system (SAP SuccessFactors) with minimum notice: 5 working days for leave of 3+ days; 1 working day for single-day requests where operationally possible.

### Sick Leave
Medical certificates are required for absences of 3 or more consecutive days. German sites: Krankengeld applies after 6 weeks per German law. Other sites: local statutory sick pay rules apply. Moongas supplements statutory provisions to ensure employees receive full basic salary for up to 4 weeks of illness per year.

### Parental Leave
- **Germany**: Full statutory Elterngeld and Elternzeit entitlements apply. Moongas provides a top-up payment for the first 12 weeks of parental leave to bring pay to 100% of base salary.
- **Other sites**: Minimum statutory entitlement in each country, with Moongas matching at least 8 weeks full pay for primary caregivers.

## 6. Health and Safety

Safety is a non-negotiable value at Moongas. All employees are required to:
- Complete site safety induction before commencing any work on a production floor or engineering area
- Wear appropriate PPE (personal protective equipment) at all times in designated areas
- Report unsafe conditions, near-misses, or incidents immediately to their line manager and the Safety Officer
- Never operate machinery without appropriate certification and authorisation
- Refuse to carry out an instruction that they believe creates an unsafe condition — this right is protected

**Incident reporting:** All incidents (injuries, near-misses, property damage) must be recorded in the safety management system within 4 hours. Serious incidents (LTI — Lost Time Injury) must be reported to Gerhard Weiß (COO) and the site Safety Officer within 1 hour.

**Zero Tolerance:** Safety violations — including bypassing machine guards, removing lockout/tagout devices, or operating under the influence — result in immediate disciplinary action and may result in termination.

## 7. Code of Conduct

All Moongas employees are expected to:
- Treat colleagues, customers, and suppliers with respect, irrespective of seniority or nationality
- Protect confidential information — customer drawings, pricing, manufacturing processes, and commercial contracts are strictly confidential
- Not accept gifts from suppliers or customers above EUR 35 in value without manager approval
- Avoid conflicts of interest — disclose any personal interest in a supplier or customer relationship to HR immediately
- Report suspected corruption, fraud, or bribery via the Moongas Ethics Hotline (ethics.moongas.com) — available in German, English, Polish, Japanese, and Portuguese

Moongas is signatory to the UN Global Compact and the German Association of the Automotive Industry (VDA) Code of Conduct for Suppliers.

## 8. Performance Management

### Annual Review Cycle
Performance reviews are conducted in November–December for the preceding 12-month period. Results inform March salary adjustments and annual bonus payments. Rating scale: Exceptional / Exceeds Expectations / Meets Expectations / Partially Meets / Does Not Meet.

### Improvement Plans
Employees rated "Partially Meets" or below receive a structured 90-day improvement plan with specific, measurable targets. Continued underperformance results in escalation to formal disciplinary process.
